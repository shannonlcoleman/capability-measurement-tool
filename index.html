The Capability Question

A research design tool for AI enablement measurement

Most organizations deploying AI tools to their workforce are measuring adoption. Utilization rates. Completion data. Satisfaction scores. None of that tells you whether your people are actually getting better at their jobs.

This tool asks you 19 questions about your AI tool, your employees, and your organizational context. Then it generates a tailored study design, honest about what is feasible and specific about what the findings can and cannot claim.
